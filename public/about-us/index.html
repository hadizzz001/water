Company Profile

CRYO TECH & WATER&POOLS

Since 2008, CRYO TECH & WATER&POOLS has been a leading provider of water engineering technology solutions. Founded in Lebanon and expanding to Muscat, we are driven by a commitment to excellence and a strong partnership with international companies in the USA, Europe, and Australia.

Under the direction of Engineer Hassan Jammal, a specialist in water engineering technology solutions, we offer comprehensive services including:

  • Process engineering solutions
  • High-quality designs and execution
  • Reliable after-sales service

We specialize in building, designing, and maintaining water filtration systems, swimming pools, saunas, dancing and musical fountains, steam rooms, sewage treatment, greywater systems, and water features for residential, commercial, and governmental projects.

Our dedication to providing exceptional customer service and building long-term relationships sets us apart.
